Karrow LLC was founded to offer practical, flexible, business & transaction oriented legal services to corporate & individual clients. Our approach is rooted in our real-world experience as investors, entrepreneurs, and capital market participants, and we bring the lessons we've learned to every client matter.

Why We're Different

We are Business People

We were business people before we were lawyers. We understand the law, but we also understand the businesses of our clients, so we're able to see problems the way they see them.

We are Flexible & Rational

Everything about us - from the way we charge, to the perspective with which we view our clients problems, to our unique myGC program - puts our interests in line with the client's.

We're Responsive

We're always on call for clients. If it's important enough for a client to reach out, it's important enough for us to be available.

About the Founder

David Karrow founded Karrow LLC in 2016 after spending over a decade in the capital markets and private investment industries.

"I've spent my career investing in early and growth stage companies, trading derivatives, and participating in the management of portfolio companies. My experience in business sets me apart from other attorneys, because the ability to come up with good legal solutions is less valuable when those solutions aren't informed by an acute understanding of a client's business objectives."

General Corporate & Transactions

As experienced entrepreneurs and business people, we've led companies as they navigate the exciting life-stages of formation, capital raising, and expansion. We've advised on and crafting employee equity compensation plans, technology license agreements, and corporate operating documents, and guided companies through the investment and sale processes.

  • We have:
    • Represented financial technology companies in sales to strategic acquirers;
    • Represented investors in a variety of industries, including financial technology, real estate, hospitably, and manufacturing.
    • Represented participants in technology-related joint-ventures.
    • Structured secured debt investments in manufacturing and real estate related businesses.
    • Negotiated complex technology licensing arrangements.

Futures, Securities, & Derivatives

Financial services firms face increasingly complex legal, business, and regulatory challenges. Our background in finance, and understanding of proprietary trading, hedge funds, and asset management give us unique perspective to help solve our clients' most pressing needs.

  • We represent:
    • Proprietary Trading Firms & Individual Traders;
    • Introducing Brokers & Futures Commission Merchants (FCMs);
    • Commodity Pool Operators & Commodity Trading Advisors;
    • Broker Dealers & other asset managers;

Litigation, Arbitration, & Mediation Advisory

When conflicts arise in business, determining the best course of action to find a solution is often stressful and time consuming. Finding creative solutions to conflicts, or managing litigation strategy and expenses, requires attorneys who understand the possible outcomes from the perspective of their clients.

We have represented clients in disputes arising out of closely held businesses, investments in privately held companies, and general breach of contract matters. Regardless of the dispute, our goal is to solve the problem for our clients in the most efficient way possible.
